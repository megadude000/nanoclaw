### 1.2 Key Observations

- **Engagement vs. reach trade-off**: Larger CZ coffee accounts (>10K) show declining engagement rates (1.4%–2.8%). Micro-accounts under 5K consistently outperform with 5%–6% engagement.
- **Czech-language content wins locally**: Accounts posting in Czech, even with occasional English, show 30–40% higher comment volume from local audience.
- **Reels are underleveraged**: Most CZ coffee accounts still rely on static imagery. Rebel Roasters and Filtrkava are exceptions — and they're growing fastest.
- **Founder voice is differentiator**: Kavarna Co Hledas and Na Opici show that personal narrative beats polished production for small brands.

## 2. YourWave Content Pillars

Based on competitive analysis and YourWave's positioning (atlas + DTC + education), the following 5 content pillars are recommended.

### Pillar 1: Origin & Terroir — "Odkud káva přichází"
**Rationale**: Specialty coffee differentiation lives in origin. This pillar establishes authority and justifies premium pricing.

Content types:
- Origin country cards (Ethiopia, Colombia, Kenya, Guatemala) with flavor profiles
- Processing method explainers (natural / washed / honey)
- Farm photography and supplier relationship content
- "Why this lot?" micro-essays in captions

CTA: "Explore the atlas → [link in bio]"

### Pillar 2: Craft & Brewing — "Uvař lépe"
**Rationale**: Brewing education creates habitual value — followers return for tips. High save rate = algorithmic signal.

Content types:
- V60 / AeroPress / Moka Pot recipe Reels (30–60 sec)
- Ratio cards (static or carousel)
- "Common mistakes" educational carousels
- Equipment comparisons at accessible price points

CTA: "Save this for your next brew ☕"

### Pillar 3: The Wave — Community & Culture
**Rationale**: Brand community is the moat. This pillar converts followers into subscribers.

Content types:
- Subscriber spotlights / UGC reposts
- "Coffee with" — founder pairing coffee with activities (reading, coding, morning walks)
- CZ coffee culture moments (café visits, local events)
- Behind-the-sourcing Stories

CTA: "Join the wave → subscription link"

### Pillar 4: Transparency — "Jak to děláme"
**Rationale**: DTC brands win when they show the process. Trust = conversion.

Content types:
- Sourcing decision transparency (why we chose this roaster / skipped another)
- Pricing breakdowns ("where your €18 goes")
- Packaging sustainability updates
- Tasting notes process (how we cup and score)

CTA: "Ask us anything in DMs"

### Pillar 5: Czech Coffee Life — "Kávová republika"
**Rationale**: Localization builds belonging. Czech coffee culture is distinct — lean into it.

Content types:
- Czech café of the week (collab opportunity)
- Local coffee events coverage
- Czech specialty roaster spotlights (collaborative, not competitive framing)
- Seasonal CZ content (Advent coffee rituals, summer terrace brewing)

CTA: "#YourWaveCZ — tag your brew"

## 3. Posting Frequency Recommendations

### Recommended Weekly Cadence (Early Stage, <6 months)

| Format | Frequency | Rationale |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Feed Posts (Static/Carousel) | 3x/week | Foundation for grid aesthetic; high save rate |
| Reels | 2x/week | Growth lever; reach new audience outside followers |
| Stories | 5–7x/week (daily) | Community touchpoint; low-effort, high retention |
| Lives | 1x/month | Deep engagement; cupping sessions or Q&A |

**Total weekly output**: ~15–20 Story slides + 5 feed/reel posts

**Note**: Quality over quantity. For a solo founder / small team, 3 feed posts + 2 Reels + daily Stories is sustainable and outperforms brands posting daily mediocre content.

### Scaling (6–12 months, with traction)
- Increase Reels to 4x/week
- Add Collab posts (3–4/month with partner accounts)
- User-generated content reposts can fill 1 feed slot/week

## 7. Influencer Collaboration Approach for CZ Market

### Tiered Approach (aligned with <€500/month budget)

#### Tier 1: Micro-influencers (2K–15K followers) — PRIMARY FOCUS
- **CPA/CPE**: €0.02–0.08 per engagement; often works for product exchange
- **Profiles to target**: Czech lifestyle bloggers, food photographers, WFH content creators, home barista enthusiasts
- **Ask**: Unboxing or brewing Reel in exchange for 1-month coffee subscription (€25–35 value)
- **Target**: 3–5 micro-collab posts/month
- **Example profiles**: Czech WFH lifestyle, Prague food scene, Czech minimalist living

#### Tier 2: Nano-influencers (500–2K followers) — COMMUNITY BUILDING
- Almost always willing to collaborate for free product
- Focus on authenticity over reach — these followers trust recommendations
- Ideal for UGC that you can repost with permission

#### Tier 3: Coffee-specialist accounts — CREDIBILITY PLAY
- Partner with Czech barista champions or certified Q-Graders for one educational collab/quarter
- Position as: "We asked a certified Q-Grader to cup our latest lot..."
- High credibility signal; lower reach but strong conversion intent

### Outreach Template Approach
- Personalized DM in Czech
- Lead with what you know about their content (specific post reference)
- Clear value proposition: free subscription + co-branded content
- No agencies needed at this stage — founder-direct outreach performs better for authenticity

### Avoid
- Mega-influencers (>100K): CPM too expensive; audience not coffee-specific
- One-size-fits-all gifting campaigns without content brief
- Influencers who post competitor coffee brands within same month

## 8. KPIs to Track

### Primary KPIs (review monthly)

| KPI | Target (Month 1–3) | Target (Month 4–6) |
|-----|-------------------|-------------------|
| Follower growth rate | +8–12%/month | +5–8%/month (as base grows) |
| Average engagement rate | >3.5% | >3% |
| Reel reach (non-followers) | >40% of total Reel views | >50% |
| Story views / follower ratio | >15% | >20% |
| Link-in-bio clicks | 50+/month | 150+/month |
| DM conversations initiated | 10+/month | 30+/month |

### Secondary KPIs (review weekly)

| KPI | Tracking Method |
|-----|----------------|
| Save rate per post | Instagram Insights → Saves |
| Best-performing content pillar | Engagement per post by pillar tag |
| Hashtag reach contribution | Insights → Impressions from Hashtags |
| Story completion rate | Swipe-away rate per Story slide |
| Profile visits from Reels | Reel analytics in Instagram Insights |

### Revenue-Connected KPIs (review monthly)

| KPI | How to Track |
|-----|-------------|
| Subscription starts attributed to Instagram | UTM link in bio → Google Analytics |
| Atlas page views from Instagram | UTM tracking on bio link |
| Promo code redemption from influencer collabs | Unique codes per collab |

### Reporting Cadence
- **Weekly**: Engagement rate, follower growth, top post
- **Monthly**: Full KPI review + content pillar performance analysis
- **Quarterly**: Strategy review, hashtag refresh, influencer audit
