## Key Considerations

### Logistics
- **Jakafe** has zero logistics friction (Prague warehouse)
- **Algrano** uses Bremen warehouse — reasonable road freight to CZ
- **Trabocca** and **InterAmerican** ship from Amsterdam/Hamburg — good corridors to CZ
- **Nordic Approach** ships from Belgium (Seabridge) — also well-connected

### Pricing Reality Check
- Green coffee commodity prices have been volatile (2025–2026)
- US tariffs (April 2025) affected Algrano pricing for US-bound coffee — less relevant for CZ/EU
- All suppliers require account creation for actual pricing — this is industry standard
- Budget for €4–8/kg for standard specialty, €8–15/kg for premium lots, €15+/kg for competition/rare lots

### EUDR (EU Deforestation Regulation)
- Takes effect January 2027
- Requires traceability to plot of land for all imported coffee
- **Belco** and **Algrano** are explicitly EUDR-ready
- Choose suppliers who are preparing now to avoid disruption later

### Currency Considerations
- Most suppliers price in EUR (good for CZ, easy conversion to CZK)
- Some (DRWakefield) primarily work in GBP — watch exchange rates
- Algrano offers multi-currency (EUR, USD, GBP, CHF)
